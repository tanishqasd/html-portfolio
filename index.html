Hey it's me Narendra Modi

Early Life and Education

Narendra Modi was born on September 17, 1950, in Vadnagar, Gujarat, India. He grew up in Vadnagar, the son of a street merchant. Modi completed his secondary education there.

Political Journey

  • He entered politics as a youth and quickly rose through the ranks of the Rashtriya Swayamsevak Sangh (RSS), a Hindu nationalist organization.

Chief Minister of Gujarat

  • Modi served as the Chief Minister of Gujarat from 2001 to 2014.

Personal Life

  • Modi was married to Jashodaben Modi in 1968, but they separated soon after.
  • Modi has a strong spiritual inclination and has often spoken about his devotion to Hinduism.
  • He has visited various temples and ashrams across India.

  • Diet and Lifestyle

  • Modi follows a strict vegetarian diet.
  • He practices yoga and emphasizes its benefits for physical and mental well-being.

Fashion and Style

  • Known for his distinctive style, Modi often wears traditional Indian attire, including kurta-pajama and Nehru jackets.
  • His signature accessory is a vibrant saffron-colored stole.

  • UAE: Order of Zayed (2019)

  • Fiji: Order of Fiji (2023)
